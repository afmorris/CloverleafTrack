Design notes — Mockup A

  • Chart appears inside the existing event group — no new UI section to learn
  • Trend indicator in header gives the story without opening anything
  • Chart is season-scoped only (one season's data) — keeps it focused
  • Downside: chart is narrow and may feel cramped on events with many performances
  • Downside: doesn't show multi-season progression — you'd need to open each season individually
